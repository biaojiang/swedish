| som ser ut att komma från ... | that appears to come from ... |

[^1]
- **"rätt så"** = "quite" or "fairly."
- **"vana vid"** = "used to" or "accustomed to."

So, **"rätt så vana vid"** means **"quite used to"** or **"fairly accustomed to."**
